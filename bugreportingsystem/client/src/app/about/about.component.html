What is E-bug ?

E-bug is the vulnerability coordination and bug bounty platform that connects businesses with penetration testers and cybersecurity researchers. Bugv is founded with the vision to improve the security infrastructures in the country. We partner with the global hacker community to surface the most relevant security issues of our customers before they get exploited by others.
